Wilderness Survival

....

Learning Objectives/Desired Outcomes

Strategy and planning, Problem solving and decision making, Resource allocation and management, Creative thinking, Collaboration and Teamwork.

Target Group:

Junior, Middle and Senior Management

Methodology:

A group is left in a  jungle to find for themselves from the natural resources which includes making fire, shelter, finding food, water and survive the night in the wilderness.

Requirements/Props:

Match boxes, Tarp, Newspapers, Twine, Shovel, Metal Coffee can, Bandana.

Time Duration:

90 minutes to 180 minutes

Number of Participants:

5-50

Preferred Setting:

Outdoor

....
